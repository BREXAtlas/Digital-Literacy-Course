# Code of Conduct

This project follows the spirit of the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/).

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of
background, identity, device access, or technical experience — fitting for a course whose subject matter
explicitly avoids shaming learners with limited devices, bandwidth, storage, or paid-software access.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive in issues, discussions, and reviews.
- Assume good faith; critique ideas and content, not people.
- Keep curriculum discussions free of judgment toward any device, budget, or technical-experience level — this
  mirrors the course's own non-judgmental teaching philosophy.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, discriminatory language, or personal attacks.
- Shaming any student's device access, technical background, or AI-use history in issues or curriculum
  contributions.
- Submitting curriculum content that violates `docs/SOURCE_POLICY.md` (biased claims, unverified facts,
  product promotion, affiliate links, or instructions for evading academic-integrity or security rules).

## Reporting

Open a GitHub issue, or contact the repository maintainers directly for sensitive reports.
