# Lesson Character and Major Map

Student names and scenarios are illustrative. Majors are current Angelo State academic programs used to provide relevant context.

Each Foundations episode and AI Quest has one primary student. The opening narrative introduces that student with a current undergraduate major, and the scenario, choices, consequences, recovery path, example, knowledge check, visual support, and completion message retain that identity. The standard **Who May Benefit** concept field remains general audience guidance and is intentionally not part of the character narrative.
